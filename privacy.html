Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

dailystoptoshop ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website dailystoptoshop.com. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

Information We Collect

When you use our review feature, we collect:

  • Google account info — your name, profile picture, and unique account identifier (provided via Google Sign-In) to display alongside your reviews.
  • Review content — the star rating and text you submit.

When you place an order, we collect:

  • Delivery details — the name, phone number, email address, postal address, and pincode you enter at checkout, so we can fulfil and deliver your order.
  • Order details — the items, quantities, and amount paid. Card and other payment credentials are entered on Razorpay's secure checkout and are never seen or stored by us.

When you browse the site, our advertising tools collect:

  • Browsing activity — pages and products you view, items you add to your bag, when you begin checkout, and completed purchases (including the order value and which products were bought).
  • Device and connection data — your IP address, browser type, and a cookie identifier, collected automatically by the Meta Pixel described below.

If you enter your email into our newsletter or spin-the-wheel offer, we collect that email address so we can send you the discount and updates.

How We Use Your Information

  • To display your name and profile picture alongside your reviews.
  • To ensure one review per user per product.
  • To allow you to delete your own reviews.
  • To process, fulfil, and deliver your orders, and to contact you about them.
  • To measure how our ads perform and to show you relevant products on Facebook and Instagram.

Data Storage

Review and order data is stored securely on Cloudflare's infrastructure, and order records are also kept in our internal order log. We do not sell or trade your personal information. We do share limited data with the service providers listed under Third-Party Services, only so they can perform these functions for us.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage to remember which reviews you have liked (to prevent duplicate likes) and to keep the items in your bag. We use session storage to keep you signed in during your browsing session.

The Meta Pixel sets its own cookies (such as _fbp) to recognise your browser across visits and connect your activity to our ads.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Sign-In for authentication. Google's privacy policy applies to the data they collect during the sign-in process: Google Privacy Policy.

We use the Meta Pixel to measure our advertising on Facebook and Instagram. It reports the browsing activity listed above to Meta, together with your IP address and a cookie identifier. Meta may link this to your Facebook or Instagram account and use it to show you our ads. See Meta's Privacy Policy.

Payments are processed by Razorpay, who receive your delivery and payment details in order to take the payment. See Razorpay's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You can delete your review at any time from the product page while signed in. If you wish to have all your data removed, contact us at Connect@dailystoptoshop.com.

You can opt out of personalised advertising in your Facebook Ad Preferences, and you can block the pixel entirely with your browser's tracking protection or an ad blocker. Blocking it does not affect your ability to browse or order.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at Connect@dailystoptoshop.com.
